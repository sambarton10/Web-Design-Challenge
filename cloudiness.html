    Cloudiness

    This plot doesn't necessarily show any trend between latitude and cloudiness. We can't say cloudiness increases or decreases when latitude get closer to the equator. Clouds tend to form in abundance in the middle latitudes 60 degrees north and south of the equator. This is where the edges of polar and mid latitude circulation cells collide and push air upward.
